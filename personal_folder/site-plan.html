The main markets are weddings, anniversaries, graduations, celebrations and business events such as conferences or award ceremonies. The contacts for this type of work may be the subjects themselves or event organizers, such as conference coordinators, hotel managers or wedding planners.

Content

Home page

The purpose of this site is to help expand the business of Hatch Photography. We want to make it easier for you to make appointments, and to keep appointments organized. We also want to advertise some of our work here, so that you will be able to see what we can provide. The services provided include portraits, family and group, engagement, bridal and groom, weddings, and receptions.

We also want to show you our affordable pricing that ranges between $50 to $150 per hour. We are here to take your picture! If you got engaged or are getting married, we are here for you. We also take family photos. If you have a major event coming up, we can take pictures to help you celebrate. We can help you take photos to help you decorate your home. We also take pictures of your sweet fur babies; we know they are an important part of your family. We are here for all your photography needs!

Images for the Home page

Weddings

You are at an exciting time in your life, and we are here to help you celebrate. As you just go engaged you probably need to get some pictures. You would at least need some for your wedding announcement. You would of course want a good picture of you new ring! We have our ideas of more traditional engagement photos, but we would also love if you any special poses you would like us to take of you guys. We can even recreate or even be there for your proposal; we will be stealthy, and they won’t even know we are there.

Most people view their wedding day as the best and most important day of their life. With that you are getting married you are going to want someone who is a professional there to capture your special day. Your wedding day is when you feel your best and also look your best. We will be sure to get photos of your beautiful venue. We will take pictures of just the two of you, and we will take some of the different groups, such as your immediate family, your friends, and of course a big picture of all of your guests!

Individual

Are you looking to decorate your home, or are you looking for some good pictures? Whatever your reason is we are here for you. It is always good to have new photos of yourself and your family. If you don’t take new photos, your old photos will feel outdated. Now those pictures are important, but it is good to add to them and have pictures from different stages of your life and your family’s lives.

We also love taking pictures of your pets! We know how important they are to you, and they are so cute! And honestly, what is better than a super cute picture of them? We are great at getting them set to take pictures, as we know this is very difficult for most people. We know that it can also be difficult in large groups of people, but we specialize in getting people set quickly as we do not want to waste your time or money.
