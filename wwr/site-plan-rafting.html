Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to help you be the best water rafter there is by helping our customers navigate rough waters by supplying them with the equipment and/or the guides they need to safely head down rivers. Also, educating those who don't have any experience on how to water raft by supplying them with coaches who are dedicated to helping them feel comfortable water rafting on their own and with the equipment that will enable them to be the best of the best.

Audience

This is everyone from every work of life; young and old, experienced and the inexperienced and many more. You don't have to wealthiest to water raft. Families who need a break from the busy lifestyle and want to have a good vacation. Those who want to disconnect from the world and just get back in touch with nature. The ones who have never gone water rafting in their life and want to give it a try safely. You don't have to worry about not knowing how to swim or not being the best swimmer because we will help you get comfortable with the waters.

Typography

Heading Font: Tiro Telugu

Paragraph Font: Roboto

Roboto is a double meaning letter, because on the one hand it presents totally geometric shapes, while other characters are made with more friendly and open strokes. In addition, it is a flexible font, which allows the text that is written using it to adapt to the available width, allowing the reader to read more calmly.

Normal paragraph example
